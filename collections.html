Collections and Public Works

This page documents works by Magnús Pálsson held in museum collections, institutional archives, and public installations. This information is compiled from institutional databases, collection catalogs, and archival sources.

Holdings include:

  • Major museum collections of artworks and documentation
  • Permanent public installations and commissioned works
  • Archive materials and photographs
  • Institutional acquisitions and donations

Háskóli Íslands (University of Iceland)

  • Hvískur — Artwork Located in Læknagarður
  • Mótun lands (The Formation of Land) — Wall installation, 2010 Department of Geology and Natural Science (Askja), tiling and engraving with text from Völuspá and Jónas Hallgrímsson poetry
  • Þiljur (Claddings) — Wall installation, 1995 Department of Physiology, three wooden panels with sculpture and engraving, text from Edda transcribed into Wingdings, carving by Hannes Lárusson

Note: These works are not yet catalogued in the Sarpur database but are confirmed installations within university buildings.
